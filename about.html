Our Story

Built by tradies, for homeowners

We started PeakHome because we'd seen too many Auckland homeowners get stung by unreliable work and opaque pricing. That had to change.

Who We Are

Our mission

PeakHome NZ was founded in 2018 by Marcus Reid, a qualified plumber who'd spent 15 years on the tools in Auckland and watched friends and family get burned by no-shows, dodgy workmanship, and bills that bore no resemblance to the original quote.

The idea was simple: build a network of tradespeople who are genuinely great at their craft, pay them well, hold them to a high standard, and make the whole experience transparent for the homeowner. No middleman markups, no bait-and-switch pricing, no chasing tradespeople for callbacks.

Today we work with a hand-picked team of tradespeople across plumbing, electrical, roofing, painting, landscaping, and general maintenance. Every one of them is licensed, insured, and has been through our vetting process. We've completed over 850 jobs across greater Auckland with a 4.9-star average rating.

Our Values
