What We Offer

Trade services for every home

Six specialist trades, all under one roof. Every job is quoted upfront — no surprises.

🔧

Plumbing

Whether you've got a dripping tap or a full bathroom refit on the cards, our plumbers handle it all. We cover blocked drains, hot water cylinder replacement, burst pipe repairs, toilet and basin installs, and dishwasher connections. All work meets NZ building code standards and comes with a 12-month workmanship guarantee.

Book a plumber →
⚡

Electrical

Our registered electricians carry out a full range of domestic electrical work — from switchboard upgrades and new circuit installs to fault finding, heat pump wiring, and EV charger installation. Safety is non-negotiable: every job is inspected and certified to WorkSafe NZ standards before sign-off.

Book an electrician →
🏠

Roofing

A sound roof is the most important thing your home has. Our roofers work across corrugated iron, long-run steel, and concrete and clay tile systems. Services include leak investigation and repair, re-roofing, ridge capping, flashing replacement, and full gutter systems. We offer free roof assessments for Auckland homeowners.

Book a roofer →
🎨

Painting

Interior and exterior painting done properly — proper prep, quality coatings, and a finish that lasts. We work with both residential and rental properties. Services include full interior repaints, weatherboard preparation and painting, fence staining, deck oiling, and colour consultation. Water-based and low-VOC options available.

Book a painter →
🌿

Landscaping

From a tidy lawn to a full garden makeover, our landscaping crew handles everything outdoors. Lawn mowing and edging, garden bed establishment, retaining walls, artificial turf installation, irrigation systems, and section clearing. We work with Auckland's challenging clay soils and can advise on drainage solutions.

Book a landscaper →
🛠️

General Maintenance

All those jobs that don't fit a single trade — we've got you covered. Deck repairs, fencing, door and window adjustments, tile repairs, caulking, minor plastering, and handyman work. Perfect for rental property managers needing regular maintenance, or homeowners with a growing list of odds and ends.

Book maintenance →
