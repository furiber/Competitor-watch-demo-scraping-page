Common Questions

Pricing FAQs

Do you charge a call-out fee on top of the hourly rate?

On the Essential plan, the standard hourly rate applies from the moment the tradie arrives — there's no separate call-out fee. On Standard, the $120 flat rate covers the first 90 minutes of labour. On Premium, all call-out fees are waived.

What if the job takes longer than expected?

We'll always let you know before going over the quoted time or scope. Any additional work is approved by you before it happens. No surprise invoices.

Are materials included in the pricing?

Materials are quoted and charged separately at cost price — we don't mark them up. You'll see a clear breakdown of labour vs. materials on every invoice.

Can I cancel the Premium subscription?

Yes — cancel any time with 30 days' notice. There are no lock-in contracts. If you cancel partway through a month, you'll receive a pro-rata refund for any unused days.
